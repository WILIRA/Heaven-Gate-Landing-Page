Heaven Gate Choir: A zone of worshipers

History

This singing company has served the people of God since 2021 in Kigali-Rwanda with a small group of people. Later, it started to grow day by day.

What we do

Since our day one, we do sing, but we have developed other services like helping the poor, preparing evangelical campaigns, and other services.

Requirements to join

Time

Our services are much time-consuming to prepare. Ready to spend your time?

Come and Join us in singing!

Donating Spirit

Among what we do, we help and support those who are needy either among us and outside. If you are ready to donate, please come.

Talented in Singing

Our main mission is singing, if you are not interested in it you can be lost. This is why we prefer those who are singers.
